What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. It's just as important as physical health and can be improved with care and understanding.

How to Tackle Mental Health Challenges

  • Speak with a trusted friend or therapist
  • Practice regular self-care and mindfulness
  • Exercise and get enough sleep
  • Maintain healthy social connections
  • Seek professional help when needed

  • Reasons to Stay Alive – Matt Haig
